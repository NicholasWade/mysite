This is what my 2013 Kawasaki Ninja 300 ABS looked like after my accident. I was riding north down Cuming St on my way to work it had just sprinkled earlier in the day. I was coming upon Martha St where there were building blocking my view of oncoming traffic to the east. The light turned green as I was approaching the intersection and a person ran the red light trying to make a last minute turn far too late from the light changing. After he had pulled out into the intersection, instead of following through with his poor decision which he would have been fine if he did, he decided to stop in the middle of the road. I immediately reacted by slamming on my breaks which didnt work due to the ground being slightly wet and I slid into him causing me to flip off my bike head first into his car. The funny thing is that as I was laying there on the ground waiting for the ambulance, my dad had accidentally driven passed me without even knowing it was me on the ground because he wasn't able to get a good look at me or the bike that was involved in the crash.
